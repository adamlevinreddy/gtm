## Notes
- Some large companies (Verizon, Cisco) have both buyer and vendor divisions attending. When in doubt, check the attendee's title — if they're in sales/marketing/solutions/partnerships, they're vendor-side. Verizon's main brand (3 attendees: Sr. Director x2, Consultant) may be prospect-side — review case by case.
- Duplicate company names with slight variations (trailing spaces, abbreviations) are listed separately to catch all variants in data matching.
- This list should be reviewed and updated after each conference.
- All excluded contacts should be exported to a separate "Vendors" sheet for ad platform blacklists.
